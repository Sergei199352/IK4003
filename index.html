Privacy Policy for 4003

Last updated: May 15, 2024

This privacy policy describes how our mobile application, 4003 (hereafter "App"), collects, handles, and uses the information gathered from users. The App connects to a 4003 device via Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) and is designed to collect data regarding device configurations, ambient magnetic fields, and other events detected by the device.

Information Collection and Use

The App collects the following information from the connected 4003 device:

  • Device configurations
  • Ambient magnetic field measurements
  • Event data collected by the device

No personal information is collected from the users. All data collected is strictly related to the device and its environment.

Permissions Required

The App requires the following permissions:

  • Location access: Required for establishing a connection over BLE. Note: The App does not record or track your location.
  • File writing access: Necessary for saving data onto your device's storage.

Storage of Information

All data collected by the App is stored securely in a database. The information is used solely for the purpose of functioning and improving the App and is not shared with any third parties.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
